# Content And Copy

## Copywriting Principles

Write for skim readers:

- Lead with outcomes, not internal process.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use concrete numbers.
- Avoid generic phrases like "comprehensive range of solutions" unless followed by specifics.
- Make CTAs active and specific.
- Sound professional, not inflated.

## FAQ Draft

### What does Americall do?

Americall provides calling, appointment-setting, campaign support, CRM/dialer coordination, and administrative support for businesses that need more qualified sales conversations.

### Which industries does Americall serve?

Americall works with U.S. businesses across solar, roofing, HVAC, real estate, and other home improvement categories.

### Can Americall work with our existing CRM?

Americall can support campaigns around dialer and CRM workflows. The exact setup should be confirmed during the demo based on your current tools.

### How does Americall qualify appointments?

Agents follow campaign-specific scripts and qualification rules designed around your offer, target customer, and sales process.

### Can the website content be edited later?

Yes. Phase 1 can launch as a static site, and Phase 2 can add a CMS so the owner can update services, FAQs, team profiles, images, and voice clips.
