# Project Context

## Business

Americall is a BPO/call-center business focused on calling solutions, appointment setting, lead handling, and sales support for U.S. businesses. The old website points especially toward solar, roofing, HVAC, real estate, and home improvement companies.

The website is mainly an official business presence and lead-generation landing site. It does not need app-like functionality in Phase 1.

## Target Users

Primary user:

- U.S. business owner, sales manager, or operations manager.
- Likely in solar, roofing, HVAC, real estate, or home improvement.
- Wants more qualified appointments, better call coverage, and fewer wasted sales trips.
- Has limited time and will skim quickly.
- Needs to trust the company before booking a demo.

Secondary user:

- Potential employee/agent or partner researching legitimacy.
- Existing client checking contact details.

## User Need

The visitor needs to understand quickly:

- What Americall does.
- Which businesses it serves.
- Why it can be trusted.
- What results it has delivered.
- How to contact/book a demo.

## Old Website Observations

The `Old Website/` folder contains screenshots of the previous site. Preserve these files as reference material.

Observed structure:

- Header with logo and nav: Home, Services, FAQ, About Us, Contact Us.
- Hero section with dark teal background, serif/italic typography, phone image, and "Discover More" CTA.
- Services section with four cards:
  - Dialer & CRM Solutions
  - Skilled Agent Recruitment & Training
  - Strategic Campaign Management
  - Dedicated Administrative Support
- Key Workforce Statistics section with six metrics.
- Sales success section focused on solar, roofing, real estate, and home improvement.
- Testimonials from business owners.
- Book a Free Demo form.
- Footer with address, email, website, nav links, Facebook, Instagram, and copyright.

Main improvement opportunities:

- Replace dated serif/italic-heavy typography with modern sans-serif hierarchy.
- Reduce the one-color dark teal visual monotony.
- Use stronger above-the-fold messaging and a clearer CTA.
- Use authentic, polished imagery rather than generic grayscale/cropped stock.
- Improve mobile-first spacing, form usability, and scannability.
- Make metrics feel credible and easy to understand.
- Add better accessibility and SEO structure.

## Owner Requirement

The owner may later want to update content themselves. Phase 1 should still start static, with a clean path toward a future CMS.

The owner wants the About page to include:

- Agent images.
- One-line quotes.
- Voice recordings that play when clicked or interacted with.

Implementation note: use click/tap as the primary trigger. Hover may be an enhancement only, not the main interaction.

Current asset decision:

- Use placeholder agent images and placeholder/sample audio metadata during initial build.
- Replace placeholders with real owner-provided agent photos and voice clips before launch.
- Do not imply placeholder people are actual Americall agents.
